Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my expenses?
To start tracking expenses, simply tap the '+' button in the app and enter the details of your purchase. You can categorize expenses for better insights.
Can I set multiple financial goals?
Yes! CoinWealth allows you to set and track multiple financial goals simultaneously. Go to the 'Goals' section to add and manage your objectives.
How does the "Should I Buy It?" feature work?
This feature helps you make informed purchasing decisions. Enter the item's cost and details, and the app will analyze its impact on your budget and financial goals.
